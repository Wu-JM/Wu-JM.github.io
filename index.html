PhD Student • Laboratory of Solid-State Physics • Université Paris-Saclay

Hi! My name is JIAMING WU. I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Laboratory of Solid-State Physics at Université Paris-Saclay. My thesis work focuses on Artificial Intelligence Hardwares, especially Neuromorphic Computing, with the supervision of Dr. Marcelo J. Rozenberg (LPS Orsay, CNRS) and Dr. Olivier Schneegens (GeePs, CNRS).

My research interests include but are not limited in:

  • Neuromorphic Computing
  • AI Hardware
  • Spiking Neural Networks
  • Ring Attractors
